About This Tracker

This tracker displays a real-time simulation of U.S. material costs following the June 21, 2025 airstrikes on Iran. It excludes indirect costs (e.g., manpower, logistics) and focuses solely on weapons, aircraft, and fuel.

Material Cost Breakdown

  • 6 × GBU‑57 bunker busters (~$3.5M each) → $21M
  • 20 × Tomahawk missiles (~$2M each) → $40M
  • B‑2 bomber flight time (40 hrs @ $135K/hr) → $5.4M
  • Aerial refueling (40 hrs @ $19.9K/hr) → $0.8M

Total estimated material cost: ~$67.2 million

Per-Second Rate

Spread across a 24-hour day (86,400 s), this yields an estimated burn rate of ~$778 per second.
